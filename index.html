About us

RWMBC a great destination to ride, hook up with some local riders and experience some new trails even if you're a local too. We made up of enthusiastic riders who enjoy the social, recreation and sporting aspects offered by mountain biking.
Established in 1998, Rock Wallaby Mountain Bike Club (RWMBC) is the largest mountain bike club in Nowendoc. Riders of any age are welcome to join. Our current membership includes riders from age nine through to fifty eight.
RWMBC offers a variety of competitive and social activities for riders of all ages and abilities. The club runs about 70 racing events each year, ranging from weekly XC to the biggest 72 hour race in the world, the Nowendoc to Gloucester Freeride.
